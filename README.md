# WSRA - Workshop Registration and Attendance System

Welcome to the WSRA platform! This guide provides everything you need to know about managing your workshops, coordinating attendees, and leveraging our real-time engagement tools.

## 🌟 Platform Overview

WSRA is a comprehensive workshop management solution designed to make your events professional, engaging, and data-driven. Whether you are hosting a small breakout session or a large corporate seminar, WSRA provides the tools to manage everything from the first registration to final feedback.

## 🚀 Key Features for Managers

### 📊 Executive Overview
Get a high-level view of all your events. Monitor overall registration numbers, attendance rates, and participant satisfaction scores through intuitive visual dashboards.

### 📋 Workshop Coordination
Easily create and manage workshops. You can define the number of seats, organize participants into tables and groups, and track specific designations for each attendee.

### 🎫 Automated Ticketing
Once a visitor registers, the system automatically sends a personalized workshop ticket directly to their email. These tickets include a unique QR code for seamless entry on the day of the event.

### 🤳 Real-time Attendance Scanner
On the event day, use your mobile phone as a digital scanner. Simply point your camera at an attendee's QR ticket to instantly mark their attendance and welcome them to the workshop.

### 🗳️ Live Engagement Tools
- **Real-Time Polls**: Launch interactive questions during your session and watch the results update live on screen.
- **Dynamic Leaderboard**: Keep your attendees engaged with a gamified point system that tracks participation and highlights top performers.
- **AI Feedback Analytics**: Automatically extract key themes and sentiment from attendee feedback to understand exactly what they loved about your event.

## 🔄 How to Use WSRA (Workflow)

### 1. Preparation
Start by creating your workshop in the platform. Define the venue details, seating capacity, and any specific requirements like designation-based grouping.

### 2. Registration
Share your unique registration link with your audience. They will follow a simple, 3-step sign-up process that collects all necessary information.

### 3. Communication
As people sign up, the system handles the heavy lifting. Every registered visitor receives a professional ticket with their personal details and a secure QR code.

### 4. Event Execution
On the day of the workshop:
- **Check-in**: Use the built-in QR Scanner to verify tickets and track arrivals.
- **Engage**: Use the Live Polls and Leaderboard to keep the energy high.
- **Monitor**: Check the Live Dashboard to see seating distribution and participation in real-time.

### 5. Post-Event Analysis
Review the Feedback dashboard to see sentiment trends and keyword highlights. Use these insights to make your next workshop even better!
